Sample Schedule

This is a tentative pathway for a student aspiring to major in Public & Professional Writing.

First Year

Fall Semester

  • ENGCMP 0200: Seminar in Composition - 3 credits
  • MATH 0031: Algebra - 3 credits
  • AFRCNA 0031: Introduction to Africana Studies - 3 credits
  • HAA 0010: Introduction to World Art - 3 credits

Spring Semester

  • ENGCMP 1100: Language of Business & Industry - 3 credits
  • EMGCMP 0410: Writing for the Legal Profession - 3 credits
  • COMMRC 0520: Public Speaking - 3 credits
  • AFRCNA 0127: Introduction to Africa - 3 credits

Second Year

  • HIST 0500: Colonial Latin America - 3 credits

Spring Semester
